The Cover — Why It Matters

The power of knitted fabric.

Most mattresses use woven fabric covers. We use a precision-knitted construction. This is not a marketing distinction — it changes how the mattress feels, breathes and lasts. Knitted fabric allows the latex core to do its job properly, adapting to your body rather than restricting it.

01
Flexibility for a Perfect Fit
Knitted fabric stretches and moves with the latex foam, allowing it to fully conform to your body's contours for optimal comfort and pressure relief.
02
Superior Breathability
The open structure of knitted fabric promotes continuous air circulation throughout the night — cooler and more comfortable than tightly woven alternatives.
03
Durability and Resilience
Knitted fabric is known for its strength and its ability to withstand long-term wear and tear — ensuring the mattress cover lasts as long as the latex core beneath it.
